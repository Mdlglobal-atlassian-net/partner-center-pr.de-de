### <a name="are-you-using-3rd-party-mfa-solution"></a><span data-ttu-id="9b746-190">Verwenden Sie eine MFA-Lösung eines Drittanbieters?</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9b746-190">Are you using 3rd party MFA solution?</span></span>
